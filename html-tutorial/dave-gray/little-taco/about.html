About Us

Our Story

The Little Taco Shop (LTS) was founded in 2022 with a passion for tacos. It all started with our founder's love for tacos and a dream to share that love with the community. What began as a small idea grew into a beloved taco destination.

Our Mission

At LTS, our mission is simple - to serve delicious tacos made with fresh, high-quality ingredients while providing exceptional customer service. We strive to create a welcoming atmosphere where taco lovers can come together to enjoy their favorite meal.

Our Values

  • Freshness: We believe in using the freshest ingredients in all our tacos.
  • Quality: We are committed to maintaining high standards of quality in every aspect of our business.
  • Community: We value our community and actively support local initiatives and events.
  • Sustainability: We are dedicated to minimizing our environmental impact through sustainable practices.
